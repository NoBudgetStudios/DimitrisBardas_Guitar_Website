
Dimitris Bardas was born in Trikala, Greece. His musical journey started at the age of 8 with the study of classical guitar wherein he has secured numerous awards in 
Classical Guitar Competitions held both in Greece and Europe. In 2015, Dimtitris attained his diploma in Classical Guitar.

Concurrently, at the age of 14, he delved into the flamenco guitar. Since then, his his artistic expression has been predominantly influenced by the flamenco genre. 
He has actively participated in several seminars led by esteemed flamenco guitarists, including Javier Conde and José Antonio Rodríguez.

Moreover, Dimitris has been a featured artist in various Guitar Festivals, showcasing his virtuosity both solo and in collaboration with his former mentor and soloist, Spyros Tsiolis.
