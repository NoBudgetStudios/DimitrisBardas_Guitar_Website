Dimitris Bardas is born in Trikala, Greece. He started classical guitar at the age of 8 and he has won numerous
prizes in Classical Guitar Competitions hosted in Greece. In 2011, he took the 7th place at the International Guitar Festival of Belgrade. 
He obtained his diploma in Classical Guitar in 2015 and at the age of 14, he started experimenting with flamenco. Ever since his style
